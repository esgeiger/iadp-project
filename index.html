Why accessbility matters

It's the right thing to do

The web is meant to provide access to information and opportunity to as many people as possible. It is only right that your website is accessbile to ensure that all can participate.

It's good business

As many as one in four adults in the United States have some form of disability. Do you really want to exclude one in four potential customers?

It's the law

If your website is open to the public, it's must comply with the Americans with Disabilities Act and federal rehabilitation act’s section 508, and web content accessibility guidelines (WCAG) 2.0
